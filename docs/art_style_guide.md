# Art Style Guide

Style: grounded 16-bit pixel art with readable silhouettes and clear action poses.

Rules:
- Prefer strong shape language over noisy texture.
- Keep combat actors readable against factory and street backgrounds.
- Use limited palettes with distinct values for foreground, interactables, enemies, and hazards.
- One-shot animations must communicate startup, impact, and recovery.
- Projectiles need clear direction, depth placement, and collision readability.

Avoid excessive bloom, tiny unreadable details, and props that hide player or enemy feet.
