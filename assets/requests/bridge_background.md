# Bridge Background Request

## Source Task
`tasks/ready/level3_bridge_design_and_blockout.md`

## Scenario Beat
First visit: wet river bridge near collapse, rain, chain fences, dim lamps, black water below.

## Target File Family
`bridge_first_pass_bg`

## Incoming Path
`assets/incoming/bridge/`

## Final Path After Review
`assets/final/bridge/`

## Canvas Size
384 x 216 base pixel-art canvas, exportable at 2x and 4x.

## Aspect
16:9 side-view bridge span with wide shared combat field.

## Style
Grounded 16-bit pixel art, rainy late 90s road bridge, tense but readable.

## Palette
Wet asphalt charcoal, cold river blue-black, lamp amber, rust red, pale headlight white.

## Layer Plan
- Far: dark river and distant city lights.
- Mid: bridge railings, chain fencing, lamp posts, broken span hints.
- Near: wet asphalt markings, puddle reflections, low curb shapes.
- Overlay: thin rain streaks and headlight glow, never opaque over combat lanes.

## Combat Readability
The bridge deck must clearly show near, center, and far lanes. Cracks and puddles should guide movement, not disguise hazards.

## Prompt
Grounded 16-bit pixel art background of a wet late 1990s river bridge at night, chain fences, dim lamps, rain, rusted railings, black river below, distant city lights, broken span suggested in the distance, wet asphalt with readable depth lanes for a side-view beat'em up, strong silhouettes, limited values.

## Negative Prompt
No fantasy bridge, no catastrophic gore, no modern highway, no cluttered foreground debris, no hidden feet, no full-screen rain noise.

## Review Criteria
- Bridge width and depth are legible.
- Rain atmosphere does not obscure combat.
- Collapse risk is visible without turning the scene into rubble soup.
