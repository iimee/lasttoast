# Forest Camp Background Request

## Source Task
`tasks/ready/level5_forest_camp_design_and_blockout.md`

## Scenario Beat
First visit: abandoned camp and forest fire, wet ash, wooden dorms, dining hall, smoke, rain.

## Target File Family
`forest_camp_first_pass_bg`

## Incoming Path
`assets/incoming/forest_camp/`

## Final Path After Review
`assets/final/forest_camp/`

## Canvas Size
384 x 216 base pixel-art canvas, exportable at 2x and 4x.

## Aspect
16:9 side-view camp clearing with continuous depth field.

## Style
Grounded 16-bit pixel art, damp post-fire camp, quiet and sad rather than horror.

## Palette
Wet ash gray, charred black, muted pine green, ember orange, rain blue, worn wood brown.

## Layer Plan
- Far: burnt tree line, water tower silhouette, smoke layers.
- Mid: wooden dorms, dining hall sign, sports ground remnants.
- Near: muddy path, ash puddles, low planks below foot line.
- Overlay: light rain, smoke wisps, small ember glows.

## Combat Readability
Smoke must be soft and sparse around actors. Fire patches need clear borders and must never cover all depth bands.

## Prompt
Grounded 16-bit pixel art background of an abandoned forest camp after a fire, wet ash, charred pine trunks, wooden dorm buildings, old dining hall sign, sports ground remnants, rain, smoke layers, water tower silhouette, readable side-view beat'em up floor with clear depth bands, restrained 1990s Russia mood.

## Negative Prompt
No fantasy forest, no horror bodies, no giant flames filling the frame, no noisy ash texture over the floor, no foreground planks hiding feet.

## Review Criteria
- Camp identity is visible, not just generic woods.
- Fire aftermath supports gameplay lanes.
- Mood stays human and grounded.
