## Canvas Size
384 x 216 base pixel-art canvas, exportable at 2x and 4x.

## Aspect
16:9 side-view beat'em up factory floor with readable pseudo-depth bands.

## Style
Grounded 16-bit pixel art, late 1990s Russian industrial workshop, restrained and worn, not villain-lair fantasy.

## Palette
Cold steel gray, wet concrete, rust red, old tile cream, muted amber, soft white steam.

## Layer Plan
- Far: pipes, pressure tank, tiled wall, old industrial lamps.
- Mid: conveyor shapes, valve wheels, warning stripe, factory equipment.
- Near: wet concrete floor with clear depth bands.
- Overlay: faint steam, kept sparse and never over actor feet.

## Combat Readability
Leave the lower 45% as clean walkable space with three value-separated depth bands. No prop may hide actor feet or projectile lanes.

## Prompt
Grounded 16-bit pixel art background of a late 1990s tired Russian bottling plant / brewery workshop during a worsening line accident, wet concrete factory floor, old pipes, stopped and moving conveyor shapes, valve wheels, pressure tank, tiled wall, dull warning stripe, faint steam, worn industrial lamps, readable beat'em up floor with clear depth bands, strong silhouettes, limited palette.

## Negative Prompt
No fantasy factory, no gore, no explosion spectacle, no dense steam covering the floor, no modern sci-fi machinery, no cartoon villain lair, no foreground objects covering feet.

## Review Criteria
- Factory equipment and pressure-line mood are recognizable.
- Walkable floor remains clean.
- Steam and props do not hide active combat readability.
