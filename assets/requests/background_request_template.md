## Global Rules
- Candidates must be saved under `assets/incoming/`.
- Do not move generated candidates to `assets/final/` until review accepts them.
- Keep actor feet, hitboxes, projectiles, hazards, and depth bands readable.
- Use strong silhouettes and limited values instead of tiny noisy details.
- Avoid parody, excessive gore, glamourized cruelty, and unreadable clutter.
