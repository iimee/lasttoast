# Metro Background Request

## Source Task
`tasks/ready/level4_metro_design_and_blockout.md`

## Scenario Beat
First visit: metro station and tunnel with uncontrolled train, service lamps, faded ads, route board.

## Target File Family
`metro_first_pass_bg`

## Incoming Path
`assets/incoming/metro/`

## Final Path After Review
`assets/final/metro/`

## Canvas Size
384 x 216 base pixel-art canvas, exportable at 2x and 4x.

## Aspect
16:9 side-view platform with readable walkable strip and tunnel depth.

## Style
Grounded 16-bit pixel art, old metro station, empty but still running.

## Palette
Dirty cream tile, greenish fluorescent light, dark tunnel blue, warning red, faded poster colors.

## Layer Plan
- Far: tunnel mouth, train silhouette, cable shadows.
- Mid: tiled wall, faded ads, blinking route board, service doors.
- Near: platform edge, worn floor bands, turnstile fragments.
- Overlay: flicker bands and small sparks with clear transparency.

## Combat Readability
Platform edge must be visible but not dominate. Bottom playable area needs clean lane separation and open projectile paths.

## Prompt
Grounded 16-bit pixel art background of a late 1990s metro station, faded ads on old tiles, flickering route board, dark tunnel mouth, service lamps, train silhouette, worn platform floor, empty but still operating, readable side-view beat'em up combat space with clear depth bands.

## Negative Prompt
No futuristic subway, no horror gore, no packed crowd, no excessive grime noise, no foreground posts blocking actor feet.

## Review Criteria
- Station, tunnel, route board, and platform are distinct.
- Train danger can be staged without confusing the walkable area.
- Palette separates floor, hazards, and background.
