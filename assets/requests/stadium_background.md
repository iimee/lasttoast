# Stadium Background Request

## Source Task
`tasks/ready/level2_stadium_design_and_blockout.md`

## Scenario Beat
First visit: районный стадион during a failed public event, crowd pressure, failing lights, concrete stands.

## Target File Family
`stadium_first_pass_bg`

## Incoming Path
`assets/incoming/stadium/`

## Final Path After Review
`assets/final/stadium/`

## Canvas Size
384 x 216 base pixel-art canvas, exportable at 2x and 4x.

## Aspect
16:9 side-view beat'em up background with readable pseudo-depth floor bands.

## Style
Grounded 16-bit pixel art, late 90s provincial stadium, restrained and worn, not parody.

## Palette
Cold concrete gray, faded green field paint, sodium yellow lights, muted red banner scraps, blue night haze.

## Layer Plan
- Far: dark field edge, vague apartment blocks, low fog.
- Mid: concrete stands, broken ad boards, torn banners, failing floodlights.
- Near: turnstile rail shadows and low barriers kept below actor feet.
- Overlay: sparse dust/fog and spotlight cones, low opacity.

## Combat Readability
Leave the lower 45% as clean walkable space with three value-separated depth bands. No prop may hide actor feet or projectile lanes.

## Prompt
Grounded 16-bit pixel art background of a late 1990s district stadium at night, concrete stands with peeling paint, old metal turnstiles, torn banners, failing floodlights cutting through fog, a partly cracked seating sector, damp field edge, quiet post-Soviet provincial mood, readable beat'em up floor with clear depth bands, strong silhouettes, limited palette.

## Negative Prompt
No modern arena, no festival spectacle, no gore, no cartoon criminal imagery, no dense tiny texture, no bloom-heavy lighting, no foreground objects covering feet.

## Review Criteria
- Stands, floodlights, and turnstiles are recognizable in one glance.
- Walkable floor remains clear.
- Mood supports crowd pressure without literal crowd clutter.
